I Am Legend (2007)

I am Legend follows a lone scientist in a post-apocalyptic New York City, who, accompanied by his German Shepherd, tries to find a cure for a virus that has turned most of humanity into hostile mutants. The film is based on the novel of the same name by Richard Matheson.
