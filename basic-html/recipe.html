Simple Pancakes

A recipe for fluffy pancakes

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Prepare a clean bowl
  2. Add flour, eggs, sugar, and milk inside the bowl
  3. Mix the bowl contents with a spoon
  4. Heat up the butter into liquid form
  5. Add to the liquid butter to the bowl
  6. Pour a small amount of mix in a heated pan
  7. Leave the pan on low fire for 4 minutes
  8. Flip them and let them cook another 2 minutes
  9. Server with honey

Enjoy!
